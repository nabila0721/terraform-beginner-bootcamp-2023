Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; it's okay if there are a few lumps.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
